About Us

Our Company

Here at Josh's Guitars, we have been making guitars since 2001. What started as a dream in 2000, quickly became a reality out of Josh's apartment with guitar repair and maintenance. By mid July in 2001, Josh teamed up with a local musician, Jesse "Oso" Stone, and started designing and building guitars in Jesse's barn under the name J 'N' J 'tars. In 2007, the name was changed to Josh's Guitars and opened shop in our current location. Although Jesse was no longer around, the music didn't stop and the team slowly grew from one man to the 15 employees that we currently have today.
