Hardware

When it comes to choosing the right hardware for your guitar, it can be a daunting task to try and decide what hardware will best suit your guitar and the style in which you play your music. If you know what hardware you want, then you can leave that information in the form provided when ordering. If you are one of the majority of musicians though, then just leave us a comment in the hardware section of the form that you would like to speak to our hardware specialist and we will guide you in finding the hardware that's best for you!
